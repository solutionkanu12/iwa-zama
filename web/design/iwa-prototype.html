No. You need a Stellar wallet and a phone. Iwa is built for people the banks cannot see, so a bank account is never the starting point.

No. It is a Private Proof of Reliability. A credit bureau collects and exposes your history. Iwa lets you prove you are reliable while that history stays private and stays yours.

Your record stays on your device and on chain in a form only you can open. Iwa cannot read it, and nothing is shared until you choose to generate a proof yourself.
